The PE Planning Education Show Bikathon

The PE Planning Team is taking part in a 'Bikathon' at the Education Show in March to raise money for Red Nose Day.

We have set ourselves a gruelling challenge where the team must keep the wheels of an exercise bike turning for the full three days of the show, 17th to 19th March; that's a full 24 hours on the bike in three days!!

Since we launched PE Planning in September 2010, our feet have not touched the ground, we have devoted our time to putting all our PE teaching knowledge and experience into a website for Primary School Teachers to use as a resource for their PE lessons. Since then, PEplanning.org.uk has become the number one resource in Primary Schools for PE.

The Education Show provides us with a fantastic platform to meet the people who use our resource and find out what's important to them, demonstrating our website to the thousands of people that attend the show is a great way for us to ensure we are building a resource that really suits the people who are actually using it.
